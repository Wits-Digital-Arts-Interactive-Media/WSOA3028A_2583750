Reading Reflections

Reading:Graham et al. - 2015 - Towards a study of information geographies

Information Geographies refers to the geographic distribution of information production, analysis and consumption. By studying information geographies, it allows us to gain insight into many other analytics such as patterns of access, participation and representation. Augmentations are overlays of information onto geographical areas, thus creating “Immutable Augmentations”. These are overlays that are consistent and therefore provides persistent information about an area Information geographies paints a picture not only the digital information of an area but also the power dynamics, politics and economic. For example, in an area with unstable governance and poor service delivery will result in low economic growth and less provision of services such as internet and therefore lead to low digital information accessibility from that region.

Reading: Spyros Kasapis : Internet Access as a Fundamental Right

In this new digital age that the world is rapidly evolving into, access to the internet and its capabilities is widely starting to be seen as a human right. According to the UN, “The UN Special Rapporteur on the promotion and protection of the right to freedom of opinion and expression, Frank La Rue, in his 2011 report to the UN Human Rights Council emphasized that "without Internet access, which facilitates economic development and the enjoyment of a range of human rights, marginalized groups and developing States remain trapped in a disadvantaged situation, thereby perpetuating inequality both within and between States".[6]” This led to widespread debates and arguments on grouping internet access along with basic utilities such as water and electricity and for governments to ensure their citizens have universal access Without affordable or universal access to internet, the disadvantaged and economically poorer people are separated from the markets. The coronavirus pandemic highlighted this gap, as those without internet were shut off from the online markets that were flourishing during the pandemic.
